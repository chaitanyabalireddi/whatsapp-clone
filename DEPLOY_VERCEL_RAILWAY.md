### **Step 2: Deploy Frontend to Vercel**

#### **2.1: Create Vercel Account**
1. Go to: https://vercel.com

4. Click "Deploy"

#### **2.3: Set Frontend Environment Variables**
1. Go to your frontend project dashboard
